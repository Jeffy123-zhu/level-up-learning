### Hello and Welcome to My Project!

This is a super cool online quiz application called LevelUp Learning!

Studying formulas or historical dates can be boring, right? I created this platform because I wanted to turn practicing questions into a level-up adventure! It's a single-page application (SPA)â€”you just open it and play, no downloads or installations needed.

### Core Gameplay and Goals

Challenge Quests: Choose a subject (like Math or Science), just like picking different maps in a game.

Level Up: Earn experience (XP) and see your level increase with every correct answer. Itâ€™s super satisfying to watch that progress bar fill up!
